Setting up Synchronization

CentraView allows you to synchronize your contact information with your Palm™ handheld device and Microsoft™ Outlook™ client by downloading a unique synchronization client utility to your desktop PC.  Use the following steps to synchronize your information:

  1. From the Additional menu, select Preferences.

  2. Select General from the preferences listing.

  3. Select Synchronize from the general listing.

  4. Select the link provided to download the synchronization client.

  5. Once the download is complete, run the client installation and follow the instructions presented throughout the installation process.

  6. By default, your data will be synchronized as private.  If you'd like your synchronized date to be public, de-select the checkbox.

  7. Select the Save Preferences button to save your preferences.

You may also access the Synchronize functionality by selecting the icon from the top menu bar.
