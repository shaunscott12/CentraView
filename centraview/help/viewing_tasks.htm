Viewing Tasks

You may want to remind yourself of the purpose of a task, for example, the associated project or sales opportunity, by viewing the detail.  Use the following steps to view detail on a task:

  1. From your Activities listing, select the checkbox next to the task you'd like to view.

  2. Select the View button.

    Note:     You may also click on the task title in the listing, select the icon next to the task title or view your tasks using the Calendar view.
