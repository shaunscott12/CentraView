Administering System Data

CentraView includes an extensive data administration function, which allows you to monitor what information is added, deleted and modified on your system.  CentraView also includes safeguards against data loss due to incorrect deletion of files, promotion, contacts, or any of the information included on your system.

The Data Administration functionality includes the following:

  • Garbage

  • Attic

  • Merge/Purge

  • Global Replace

  • History
