About Time Sheets

When time slips are submitted to Human Resources, they are compiled into a time sheet from which a payment is calculated.  Multiple time slips can be condensed onto one time sheet, each containing detailed information on the time and how it was spent, as well as the associated project code or support ticket.  This way, CentraView helps your to keep all your business expenses together for easy sorting.

With CentraView, you can

  • Add time sheets
