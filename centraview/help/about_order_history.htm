About Order History

When a sales opportunity presents itself, the proposal you put together with CentraView, when approved by the customer, becomes part of the order history.  Keeping clear track of your order history helps you send out proper invoices, record payments, balance expenses, track purchase orders, and keep accurate inventory.  With CentraView, you can easily record and track your order history, using the orders to produce customer invoices.  Each order entered with CentraView is automatically reflected in your inventory statistics.

With CentraView, you can

  • Add order history

  • View order history

  • Delete order history

For an example of how order history works with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
