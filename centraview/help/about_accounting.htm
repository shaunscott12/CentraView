About Accounting

One of the most confusing, and more important, aspects to all successful businesses is accurate accounting.  CentraView supplies you with a central repository to help you keep track of all your orders, invoices, payments, expenses and inventory, as well as additional specialized accounting functions.

With CentraView you can easily track the following information:

  • Order history

  • Invoice history

  • Payments

  • Expenses

  • Purchase orders

  • Items

  • General ledger accounts

  • Inventory

  • Volume discounts

  • Vendors

For an example of how accounting works with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
