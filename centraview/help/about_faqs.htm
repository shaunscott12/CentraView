About FAQs

As a workday progresses, issues are raised and debated, comments are passed back and forth, and answers are sought out.  Many of the answers to these day-to-day questions are common across the business, basic information taught to new employees over time or searched out by long-time employees in need of a refresher.

CentraView allows you to build a personalized database of frequently asked questions (FAQs) to provide quick, simple answers to the most common inquiries.  You determine which questions are most important for your business, you determine how to manage and categorize these questions and you determine the answers.

With CentraView you can

  • Add FAQs

  • View FAQs

  • Delete FAQs

  • Duplicate FAQs

For an example of how FAQs work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
