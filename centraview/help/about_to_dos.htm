About To Dos

In order to keep track all of your smaller, more individual tasks, you might use a series of written notes, posted to your bulletin board or next to your keyboard.  It's important that you have somewhere you can view them all, eliminating them from your list as you mark the progress of your day.  CentraView's ToDo listing is meant to replace these pieces of paper, giving you a central spot for all of your more personal tasks.

With CentraView you can

  • Add a ToDo

  • View a ToDo

  • Delete a ToDo

  • Duplicate a ToDo

For an example of how ToDos work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
