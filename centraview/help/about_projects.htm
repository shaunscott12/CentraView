About Projects

Projects are large-scale undertakings to strengthen your position on the sales front.  Whether the project you are involved with is the development of a product prototype, the development of marketing literature, or the installation of critical computer software, CentraView allows you to manage every step of the project process, from adding tasks to submitting timeslips and balancing project costs.

When you add tasks to a project, CentraView add these tasks to your calendar.  Any task can be considered a project milestone, and any milestone can trigger an alert or an email reminder.

With CentraView you can

  • Add projects

  • View project details

  • Delete projects

  • Duplicate projects

For an example of how projects work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
