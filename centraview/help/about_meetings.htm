About Meetings

Whether sitting down for a face-to-face or teleconferencing, meeting with your clients directly can go a long way to ensure a successful business venture.  Scheduling the meeting, alerting the attendees and securing resources are all necessary components of an effective meeting.  With CentraView, you can do all this in one central location.

With CentraView you can

  • Add a meetings

  • View a meeting

  • Delete a meeting

  • Duplicate a meeting

For an example of how meetings work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
