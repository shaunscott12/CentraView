Adding Appointments

With CentraView, you can keep all your appointments in neat order.  As you schedule appointments, CentraView adds them to your calendar, along with your other business-related activities.  Use the following steps to add an appointment:

  1. From your Activities listing, select the New button.

    Note:     You can also schedule an activity using the Calendar function by selecting the specific time or using the Schedule button.

  2. On the Details tab, enter the following information:

    • Appointment title

    • Appointment detail

    • Any associated entity

    • Any associated individual

    • Any related type

    • Any related field

    • Owner of the appointment

    • Start and end time

    • Priority

    • Whether the appointment is available for Private or Public viewing

    • Status

    • Additional notes

  3. Select the checkbox for Remind Me and enter a reminder date and time to receive a reminder of this appointment.

  4. Select the checkbox for Send Email Invitations to send email invitations to all individuals associated with this appointment.

  5. Select the Attendees tab and designate who will attend the appointment.  You may select an entire Group or individual attendees.

    Note:     You can also track response to your appointment invitation through the Attendees tab.

  6. Select the Resources tab to reserve any resources, for example, a conference room or an overhead projector, that you require for your appointment.

  7. Select the Availability tab to check the availability of the scheduled attendees.

  8. Select the Recurring tab to schedule an ongoing appointment.  An ongoing appointment is one that occurs daily, weekly, monthly, yearly or at some other frequency.

  9. Select the Attachments tab to send a file, either from the CentraView file system or from your desktop, along with the appointment notification.  You can send files as attachments or links.

  10. Select the Save and Close button to save the appointment or select the Save and New button to save and add another appointment.
