About Literature Fulfillment Requests

From time to time, customers will request additional literature on a promotion or product to raise their comfort level before ordering.  CentraView allows you to manage these information requests, tracking requests and sending the necessary information through electronic or hardcopy format.

With CentraView you can

  • Add literature fulfillment requests

  • View literature fulfillment requests

  • Delete literature fulfillment requests

  • Duplicate literature fulfillment requests

For an example of how literature fulfillment requests work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
