Adding Forecast Sales

When you identify a sales opportunity and add it to your forecasted amount, CentraView carries this information over into a sales forecast.   Forecast sales are listed on your calendar, making it easy to track their progress through to completion.  Use the following steps to add a forecast sale:

  1. From your Activities listing, select the New button.

    Note:     You can also schedule an activity using the Calendar function by selecting the specific time or using the Schedule button.

  2. On the Details tab, enter the following information:

    • Forecast sale title

    • Forecast sale detail

    • Any associated entity

    • Any associated individual

    • The source of the opportunity

    • Status

    • Stage

    • Opportunity type

    • Total amount

    • Probability as a percentage

    • Estimated close date

    • Account Manager

    • Account Team

  3. Select the Permissions tab to determine which employees may view, modify or delete the forecast sale.

  4. Select the Save and Close button to save the forecast sale or select the Save and New button to save and add another forecast sale.
