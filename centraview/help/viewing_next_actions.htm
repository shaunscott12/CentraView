Viewing Next Actions

You may want to remind yourself of the purpose of your next action, for example, the associated project, entity or individual, by viewing the action detail.  Use the following steps to view detail on a next action:

  1. From your Activities listing, select the checkbox next to the next action you'd like to view.

  2. Select the View button.

    Note:     You may also click on the next action title in the listing, select the icon next to the next action title or view your next actions using the Calendar view.
