About Forecast Sales

When opportunities are scheduled, they are assigned a tentative closing date by the sales representative.  Using this date, a sales manager can choose to view all prospective, or forecasted, sales in a calendar view or list all the sales staff activity, easily tracking opportunities and proposals for a more efficient, effective sales effort.

With CentraView, you can

  • Add forecast sales

  • View forecast sales

  • Delete forecast sales

  • Duplicate forecast sales

For an example of how forecasted sales work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
