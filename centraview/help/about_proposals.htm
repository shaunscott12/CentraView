About Proposals

When presented with a verified sales opportunity, you need to be able to put together proposals that meets the needs of the specific customer.  CentraView allows you to develop and track the progress of these proposals, each linked with an opportunity.  You may also opt to develop a proposal for each individual phase or service included in an opportunity.

With CentraView you can

  • Add proposals

  • View proposals

  • Delete proposals

  • Duplicate proposals

For an example of how proposals work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
