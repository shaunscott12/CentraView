Adding Activities to your Calendar

Keeping track of your business activities is what CentraView does best, whether they are client meetings, phone calls, appointments, larger tasks or smaller to dos.  Anytime you schedule an activity with CentraView, it is automatically added to your calendar, where you can easily view detail, get reminders and track the progress of the event.  Use the following steps to add activities directly from the Calendar view:

Note: You can also add activities to your calendar using the Activities functionality.

  1. From your Calendar listing, select the Schedule button.

  2. Select the activity to schedule from the drop-down list at the top of the screen.

  3. On the Details tab, enter the following information:

    • Activity title

    • Activity detail

    • Any associated entity

    • Any associated individual

    • Any related type

    • Any related field

    • Owner of the appointment

    • Start and end time

    • Priority

    • Whether the appointment is available for Private or Public viewing

    • Status

    • Additional notes

  4. Select the checkbox for Remind Me and enter a reminder date and time to receive a reminder of this activity.

  5. Select the checkbox for Send Email Invitations to send email invitations to all individuals associated with this activity.

  6. Select the Attendees tab and designate who will attend the activity.  You may select an entire Group or individual attendees.

    Note:     You can also track response to your appointment invitation through the Attendees tab.

  7. Select the Resources tab to reserve any resources, for example, a conference room or an overhead projector, that you require for your appointment.

  8. Select the Availability tab to check the availability of the scheduled attendees.

  9. Select the Recurring tab to schedule an ongoing appointment.  An ongoing appointment is one that occurs daily, weekly, monthly, yearly or at some other frequency.

  10. Select the Attachments tab to send a file, either from the CentraView file system or from your desktop, along with the appointment notification.  You can send files as attachments or links.

  11. Select the Save and Close button to save the activity or select the Save and New button to save the activity and add another activity.
