How do I use CentraView?

This section: Sales and Marketing

A very important part of business success is how well your marketing efforts generate sales.  Marketing events, whether contacting members of a mailing list, attending a convention or holding a promotional event, are excellent ways to generate sales opportunities.

For your ease, the How do I Use CentraView? topic is divided into sections, accessible from the link below.

 

How do I Use CentraView? Topics

Tracking Sales

CentraView gives you a quick and efficient way to track the success of your sales opportunities and proposals.  Any appointment, call, event, promotions, client meeting can generate a sales opportunity.

Sales representatives assign tentative closing date to opportunities, acting as an incentive to close the sale.

Using this closing date, a sales manager can view all forecasted sales in a calendar view or list all sales staff activity, tracking opportunities and proposals to methodically examine the sales effort.

Sales opportunities lead directly to the formation of proposals.  Proposals are often specific to a customer and are time-driven.

Planning Marketing Events

One of the most effective ways to place yourself directly in position to accumulate sales opportunities is through the use of marketing events and activities.  A marketing event entail acquiring a contact list, sending out promotional literature, or even coordinating a convention or sales event.

Marketing lists acquired generate contact information which can be used in sending out email notices, scheduling sales appointments or calls, or determining the direction of project development.

With CentraView, you can also develop personalized marketing lists that fit your customer profile.  Any marketing list is treated as a contact list for email purposes.

CentraView allows you to invite people to and track attendance at your marketing events.  Any requests for additional documentation can be tied into the marketing event, and tracked as a literature request.

CentraView also keeps track of any expenses, purchase orders or timeslips associated with events.

Any scheduled event is automatically added to your calendar, and a reminder is sent, if requested.
