About Events

One of the most effective ways to raise the awareness of your business offers is to host a marketing event.  Marketing events, whether large-scale conventions or smaller-scale individual presentations, let your prospective consumers meet your company, sample your promotions, and get company literature, as well as generate sales opportunities.  With CentraView, you can easily schedule these events, invite participants, secure any visual or marketing aids, and track how well your event aided in sales generation.

With CentraView you can

  • Add events

  • View events

  • Delete events

  • Duplicate events

For an example of how events work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
