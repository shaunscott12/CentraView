About Calendar

The Calendar allows you to manage your schedule by viewing details on your appointments and receiving alerts about all your important activities.  The Calendar allows you to view daily, weekly, monthly or yearly calendars (either current, previous or future), and provides you activity detail by simply hovering your mouse over the activity description.

Additionally, you can also browse calendars of other employees who have given you permission, allowing you to synchronize schedules, if necessary.  You can add activities directly from the calendar view or by using the Activities function.

You can also set general preferences for your calendar, that is, the refresh rate and the default view, as well as delegation preferences, which specify which employees can schedule activities to your calendar.

With CentraView, you can easily schedule any of the following activities, as well as setting individual preferences:

  • Appointments

  • Calls

  • Forecasted sales

  • Literature requests

  • Meetings

  • Next actions

  • Tasks

  • To Dos

For an example of how the calendar works with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
