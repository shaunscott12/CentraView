About Knowledge Base

The old proverb says "Knowledge is power."  Knowledge is also possibly the most important aspect of a well-functioning, communicative and efficient business force.  For easier, more common business questions, a group of frequently asked questions (FAQs) is usually more than enough information.  But when people have questions that require a greater depth of knowledge, they look for the experts.  CentraView allows you to set up your own expert knowledge database full of these in-depth answers.

With CentraView you can

  • Add to the knowledge base

  • View the knowledge base

  • Categorize the knowledge base

  • Delete from the knowledge base

  • Duplicate items in the knowledge base

For an example of how knowledge base works with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
