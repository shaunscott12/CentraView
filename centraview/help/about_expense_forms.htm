About Expense Forms

Occasionally employees will incur reimbursable expenses associated with your business.  Whether these expenses are due to mileage, hotel charges, lunch meetings or office supplies, it's important that you record and track these costs efficiently.  With CentraView, employees can enter into their reimbursable expenses through editable expense forms, speeding up both the recording and the repaying of these common business expenditures.

With CentraView you can

  • Add expense forms
