About Individuals

Individuals can be employees of companies, key contact names, or any person you deal with through your business who is part of the contact record for an entity.  Individuals can be grouped for easier identification and must be associated with an entity.

CentraView keeps track of all the essential information on specific individuals, from titles to business associations to contact information, as well as any events or activities planned with this individual.

With CentraView, you can

  • Add individuals

  • View individuals

  • Delete individuals

  • Duplicate individuals

For an example of how individuals work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
