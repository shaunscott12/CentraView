Searching for Information in CentraView

CentraView includes a powerful search capability, allowing you to search the complete database, returning instances regardless of whether the information resides in contacts, sales, marketing, or anywhere throughout CentraView.

When using a simple search from the CentraView home page, all contact information is combed to satisfy your query.  To search for specific information for other CentraView modules, simply select the tab for that informational category, enter your search information, and select the Search button.

For example, when on the home page, you enter Johnson in the search field.  The system will return all instances of Johnson throughout your contacts listing.  If you were actually searching for all sales opportunities associated with the word Johnson, you would simply select the Sales tab and activate the search from that tab.

By providing advanced search capabilities, CentraView allows you to use a search builder tool.  With the advanced search builder tool, you can determine which tables, fields or conditions to include in a search.  You can also save search characteristics for later use.
