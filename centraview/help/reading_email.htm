Reading Email

Email is a powerful and convenient tool for business communications.  With CentraView, you can read email messages sent to you via the internet.  Use the following steps to read an email message:

  1. From your Email listing, select the subject line of the email.

  2. The email message appears on the screen.

You may choose to reply to the sender, reply to all recipients, forward this message to another contact, delete the message, or store it in your folders.
