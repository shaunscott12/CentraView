About Notes

Notes are comments or explanations associated with any of the events, proposals, opportunities, activities, or any information stored in the CentraView database.  Notes may explain the result of a follow-up contact, the objective of a marketing plan, the availability of a promotion or any variety of informative analysis.  

With CentraView you can

  • Add notes

  • View notes

  • Delete notes

  • Duplicate notes

For an example of how notes work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
