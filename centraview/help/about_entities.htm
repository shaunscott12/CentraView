About Entities

Entities are the primary contact record.  For every contact entry, there is an entity record.  Entities can be companies you do business with, companies you want to do business with, or any other organization that you treat in a business sense.

Entities are associated with individuals and can be grouped into logical distinctions.  Although individuals must be part of an entity, entities do not necessarily have to contain individuals.  CentraView helps you to keep all your entity information well organized and easily accessible, from company name and contact information to proposals and marketing schemes aimed at the entity to upcoming activities involving the entity.

With CentraView, you can

  • Add entities

  • View entity detail

  • Delete entities

  • Duplicate entity information

For an example of how entities work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
