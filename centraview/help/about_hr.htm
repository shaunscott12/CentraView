About Human Relations (HR)

Maintaining a qualified and rewarded workforce is the duty of any responsible business.  With this in mind, CentraView allows you to keep all relevant Human Relations information in your database.  The Human Relations functionality allows you to add and track expense forms, process time sheets, and keep vital employee information, such as an employee listing and relevant employee handbooks.  It also includes an anonymous suggestion box to help keep the channels of communication open.

For an example of how HR works with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
