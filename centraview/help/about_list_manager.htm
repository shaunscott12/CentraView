About List Manager

One of the greatest tools associated with a sales opportunity or a promotion is a marketing list.  Marketing lists supply you with business and consumer contacts.  With CentraView, you can import marketing lists, and manage the lists by separating them into business contacts and actual customer database.

CentraView also tracks the number of opportunities generated by a marketing list, calculating the number of actual orders and the value of those orders.

With CentraView you can

  • Import a marketing list

  • View a marketing list

  • Delete a marketing list

  • Duplicate a marketing list

For an example of how list management works with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
