How do I use CentraView?

This section: Projects and Support

Based on your sales opportunities, marketing events or other business activities, you may undertake large projects to strengthen your business.  CentraView allows you to plan and track these projects.

Additionally, CentraView provides a full featured support facility, for customers and employees alike.

For your ease, the How do I Use CentraView? topic is divided into sections, accessible from the link below.

 

How do I Use CentraView? Topics

Planning Projects

When undertaking a large project, proper coordination and organization is a must.  Each project consists of individual tasks, smaller items to be completed towards the completion of the greater whole.  CentraView allows you to assign these tasks to anyone among your contacts, add the task to their calendar, notify the responsible party by email and submit timeslips for the project.  These timeslips are sent directly to Accounting, where they become part of a larger project timesheet.

Anyone associated with the project can also track the progress of a project by viewing the project detail.

CentraView also tracks any expenses associated with the project using purchase orders.

Informational Support

Informational support is something every employee and customer involved with your business will require.  CentraView allows you to store and track important business documents using a personalized file maintenance system.

Support tickets can be entered into the database to enable you to keep watch on system or business situations.  If the solution to a ticket involves an activity or event of some type, CentraView allows you schedule tasks, determine next actions or to dos, and log your resolution to the ticket.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) are entered by an administrator for the purpose of answering basic questions about business or Human Relations inquiries.  They are meant to provide quick information, while knowledge base information is more in-depth, covering a topic more completely.
