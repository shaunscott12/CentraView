About Email

CentraView includes email capabilities, similar to those found in popular email packages such as MS Outlook™ or Netscape Communicator™.  CentraView email works with all of your contacts, individuals, entities and groups, allowing you to easily and quickly determine recipients from any level of your business information.

One of CentraView's strongest features is the ability to manage multiple email accounts from one centralized email program, allowing you to determine an unlimited amount of nested folders which synchronize with personal devices, such as a Palm Pilot.  The email client also supports IMAP capability.  Please see your administrator for details on using IMAP with the mail client.

Email allows you to:

  • Read messages

  • Compose messages

  • Delete messages

  • Block senders

  • Use folders for email storage

  • Determine rules to automatically sort and store your emails

  • Set email preferences

For an example of how email works with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
