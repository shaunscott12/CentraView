About Marketing

One of the ways prospective customers can become aware of your sales and promotions is through the use of marketing events and activities.  Marketing events could be as simple as acquiring a contact list from a vendor and sending out promotional literature to enhance awareness, or as complex as coordinating a convention or sales event.

Any of these marketing methods place you squarely in front of your consumers, generating appointments, call opportunities and sales.
