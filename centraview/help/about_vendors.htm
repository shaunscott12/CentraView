About Vendors

The vendors your company works with are your business contacts.  CentraView stores these vendors as entities, and allows you to easily associate them with sales, promotions, orders, purchases and any variety of business transactions.

With CentraView, you can

  • Add vendors

  • View vendors

  • Delete vendors

For an example of how vendors works with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
