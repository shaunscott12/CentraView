Duplicating Sales Opportunities

Many of your sales opportunities could be very similar in content, whether including the same customer entity, proposal, project or activity information.  Having the ability to duplicate a sales opportunity, and make the appropriate changes, can save you time and effort.  Use the following steps to duplicate a sales opportunity :

  1. From your Opportunity listing, select the checkbox next to the opportunity you'd like to duplicate.

  2. Select the Duplicate button.

  3. A record opens with the opportunity information populated for your convenience.  Change the opportunity title and any additional information.

  4. Select the Save and Close button to save and return to the Opportunity listing or select the Save and New button to save and duplicate another opportunity.
