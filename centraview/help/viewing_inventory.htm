Viewing Inventory

You may want to remind yourself of the specifics of the inventory, such as the amount of each item on hand or the manufacturer, by viewing the detail.  Use the following steps to view detail on an inventory listing:

  1. From your Inventory listing, select the checkbox next to the inventory you'd like to view.

  2. Select the View button.

    Note:     You may also click on the inventory title in the listing or select the icon to view the detail.
