About Contacts

Contacts allows you to easily store, update and access all of your vital customer contact and business communication records.  Whether the contact is a key individual, a company or a group of manufacturers, the ability to access and manage this information is a vital component of your business success.

You can display all of your contacts or specialize your contact listing by selecting an available marketing list from the drop down box in the side bar.  Selecting a marketing list updates your contact listing, allowing you to see only the contacts vital to your current opportunity.

Contacts consist of three categories:

  • Entities

  • Individuals

  • Groups

For an example of how contacts work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
