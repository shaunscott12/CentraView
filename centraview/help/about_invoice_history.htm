About Invoice History

When you are ready to deliver an order to a customer, it's time to produce an invoice for payment.  An invoice lists clearly for the customer the goods contained in the order, the quantity included, and the full price.  With CentraView, you can easily produce invoices from the associated order history.

With CentraView, you can

  • Add invoice history

  • View invoice history

  • Delete invoice history

For an example of how invoice history works with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
