About Next Actions

With today's busy business world, coordinating what to do next to secure a business opportunity can be a complicated affair.  CentraView allows you to schedule, assign and track your next actions in regards to sales opportunity, proposal, project, or any other business activity in one central location.

With CentraView you can

  • Add a next action

  • View a next action

  • Delete a next action

  • Duplicate a next action

For an example of how next actions work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
