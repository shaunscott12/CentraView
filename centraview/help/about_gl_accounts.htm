About General Ledger Accounts

A general ledger account contains all of the financial accounts of a business, including payment and expense information.  A general ledger account helps you log and track every credit and debit by type, project relevance, employee, marketing event, or any other designation that makes business sense.  CentraView allows you to view and delete your linked general ledger accounts and is compatible with most accounting packages.

For an example of how general ledger accounts works with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
