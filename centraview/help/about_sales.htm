About Sales

The lifeblood of any business is the sale.  Sales represent revenue, increased visibility and public awareness.  When presented with a verified sales opportunity, you need to be able to put together proposals that meets the needs of the specific customer.  With CentraView, you can manage all your dynamic sales information, from composing and offering sales opportunities to your customers, to creating and tracking your proposals, to scheduling follow ups with your sales contacts, right on up to sending sales information to accounting.

For an example of how sales work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
