About Activities

During the course of a business day, many events can be happening simultaneously, whether they've been scheduled for you or not.  A good organizational system is vital to fulfilling all your business commitments, whether it's remembering to call a supplier on a price quote or being on time and prepared for a meeting with a customer.

CentraView allows you to schedule activities, request reminders, send invitations to attendees, reserve necessary resources, track results and issues, and plan your next steps, all in one simple central location.

Activity types are:

  • Appointments

  • Calls

  • Forecast Sales

  • Literature Requests

  • Meetings

  • Next Actions

  • Tasks

  • To Dos

For an example of how activities work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
