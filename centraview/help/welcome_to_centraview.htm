Welcome to CentraView
"One Business.  One Software."

CentraView provides growing businesses with that elusive centralized view of all customer and business information. Through an intuitive browser interface, CentraView delivers unparalleled "out of the box" functionality, ease of use, limitless scalability, and it's built on the industrial strength JAVA (J2EE) platform. Best of all, CentraView is extremely cost effective for businesses of all sizes. Our "no assembly required" software includes powerful contact management, communications and group scheduling, as well as document management, salesforce automation, project management, customer service, accounting and human resources modules.
