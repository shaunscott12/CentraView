About Items

Every business has something available for sale, whether it's services or actual goods.  Whatever your company has for sale is considered an item.  With CentraView, you can enter your items into the tracking system, assign price structures, check on the available inventory, and calculate cost versus price to balance expenses against profit.

With CentraView, you can

  • Add items

  • View items

  • Delete items

For an example of how items works with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
