Using the CentraView Homepage

The customizable CentraView homepage is your gateway to business management, allowing you to view your important calendar appointments, email folders, activities, opportunities, tasks or any information stored in CentraView.  From this starting point, you can quickly and easily accomplish the following:

  • Keep track of all your important business contacts

  • Receive and manage your communications using email

  • Schedule and announce all your business activities, such as, important meetings or phone calls

  • View all important events in a calender

  • View, record and share notations about business events, sales proposals or marketing strategies

  • Manage your documents using a company-wide file system

  • Publish Company News.

  • Track your outstanding sales proposals and opportunities

  • Watch the progress of your specific marketing ventures

  • Determine and assign project work

  • Lend company-wide support through FAQs and knowledge sharing

  • Transmit sales figures, expenses and other financial information directly to Accounting

  • Store important Human Resources employee information and support documents
