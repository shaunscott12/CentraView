Composing Email

With CentraView, you can compose email messages to be sent to anyone on your contact list or in your address book.  Use the following steps to compose an email message:

  1. From your Email listing, select the Compose button.

  2. Enter an email address, or select the To: button to access your mailing list and select recipients.

  3. Enter your subject line and the body of your message in the spaces provided.

  4. Select the Attach File button to send a text or image file with the email message.

  5. Select the Send button.
