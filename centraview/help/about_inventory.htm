About Inventory

It's very important that your business keep it's inventory current.  CentraView makes it easy to record all of your inventory, refresh your existing inventory, as well as track your inventory to incoming orders and payments.

With CentraView, you can

  • Add inventory

  • View inventory

  • Delete inventory

For an example of how inventory works with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
