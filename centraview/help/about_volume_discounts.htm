About Volume Discounts

Your business can use volume discounts as part of your promotion or marketing plans, to attract new customers or reward loyal ones.  Volume discounts designate a price break at a certain amount or inventory ordered.  CentraView allows you to determine discount amounts, designate which customers are available to receive the discount, and how long the discount is available.

With CentraView, you can

  • Add volume discounts

  • View volume discounts

  • Delete volume discounts

For an example of how volume discounts works with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
