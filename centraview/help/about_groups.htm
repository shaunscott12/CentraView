About Groups

Contacts can be sorted into groups for easier association.  Groups are made up of individuals.  The grouping of these contacts is completely at the judgement of the user.  For example, you can group individuals based on whether they resell hosting services or stock your current product line, or any other distinction that makes logical sense for your business.

However, when you add an entity to a group listing, only the individuals for the entity, not the entity name itself, are added to the grouping.  Grouping your contacts can be very helpful for authorization security, managing email lists, or identifying project teams.

With CentraView, you can

  • Add groups

  • View groups

  • Delete groups

  • Duplicate groups

For an example of how groups work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
