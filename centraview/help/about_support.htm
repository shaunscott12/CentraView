About Support

The complexity of today's advanced business world renders support facilities absolutely essential to proper functioning and knowledge sharing.  A support network can be comprised of tickets describing business challenges or problems, frequently asked questions (FAQs) for quick information, or more in-depth, critical knowledge for expert analysis.  With CentraView, you can design a fully functioning support system to satisfy every informational inquiry.
