How do I use CentraView?

This section: Accounting and Human Resources

Part of a successful business is keeping your accounting in order and keeping your employees satisfied and informed.  All your efforts towards generating sales opportunities, putting together proposals, scheduling activities or completing projects would mean very little if you let your accounting practices send you into a financial spiral.  With CentraView, accounting is made simple.

CentraView also includes easy Human Relations coordination, allowing easy access to employee information and paycheck tracking.

For your ease, the How do I Use CentraView? topic is divided into sections, accessible from the link below.

 

How do I Use CentraView? Topics

Accounting

Once you have verified sales opportunities and developed proposals, you must submit an order.  CentraView allows you to track your order history, log responses to your invoices, and offer volume discounts, as well as record any payments received for delivered orders.  Using a third party accounting package, you can link all this financial information using a general ledger account.

As well as customer financial information, CentraView tracks your business expenses and purchase orders.  As important as orders is your ability to fulfill them from available inventory.  Adding items to your inventory, managing the physical location of your goods, and tracking vendor response to inventory responses can all be managed through CentraView.

Human Resources Support

Maintaining a qualified and rewarded team of employees is the duty of any responsible business.  With this in mind, CentraView allows you to keep all relevant Human Relations information in your database.  The Human Relations functionality allows you to add and track expense forms, process time sheets, and keep vital employee information, such as an employee listing and relevant employee handbooks.  It also includes an anonymous suggestion box to help keep the channels of communication open.
