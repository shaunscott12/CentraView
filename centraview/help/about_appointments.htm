About Appointments

The ability to keep appointments is a key factor in how your company is seen by customers.  With CentraView, you can keep all your appointments in neat order.  With a singular effort, you can send reminders and invitations to vital attendees, track the progress made during the appointment, and tie your appointment results into your sales, promotions and marketing efforts.

With CentraView you can

  • Add appointments

  • View appointments

  • Delete appointments

  • Duplicate appointments

For an example of how appointments work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
