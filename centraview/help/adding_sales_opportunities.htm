Adding Sales Opportunities

As you schedule and record sales opportunities, CentraView automatically adds them to your calendar.  This way, your sales managers and sales force can track the progress of these forecasted opportunities and generate proposals accordingly.  Use the following steps to add a sales opportunities:

  1. From your Opportunity listing, select the New button.

  2. Enter the following information:

    • Opportunity title

    • Opportunity detail

    • Any associated entity

    • Any associated individual

    • The source of the opportunity

    • Status

    • Stage

    • Opportunity type

    • Total amount

    • Probability as a percentage

    • Estimated close date

    • Account manager

    • Account team

  3. Select the Save and Close button to save the opportunity or select the Save and New button to save and add another opportunity.
