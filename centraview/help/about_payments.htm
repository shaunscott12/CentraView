About Payments

Once a customer receives an order and an invoice, it is expected that they will soon remit payment.  Customer payments help cover business expenses, payroll, and other operating costs.  CentraView allows you to set the payment options your business will accept, efficiently record partial payments, track payments to all associated invoices and calculate remaining amounts.

With CentraView, you can

  • Add payments

  • View payments

  • Delete payments

For an example of how payments work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
