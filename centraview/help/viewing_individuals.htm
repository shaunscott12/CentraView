Viewing Individuals

You may want to remind yourself of the specific entity or group information associated with an individual by viewing the individual detail.  Use the following steps to view detail on an individual:

Note: You can schedule any business activity for an individual while viewing the detail using the drop down list provided.

  1. From your Individual listing, select the checkbox next to the individual you'd like to view.

  2. Select the View button.

    Note:     You may also click on the individual title in the listing or select the icon to view the individual detail.
