Adding ToDos

In order to keep track all of your smaller, more individual tasks, you should add them to your To Do list in CentraView, where they are automatically added to your calendar.  Use the following steps to add a To Do:

  1. From your Activities listing, select the New button.

    Note:     You can also schedule an activity using the Calendar function by selecting the specific time or using the Schedule button.

  2. On the Details tab, enter the following information:

    • To Do title

    • To Do detail

    • Any associated entity

    • Any associated individual

    • Any related type

    • Any related field

    • Owner of the appointment

    • Start and end time

    • Priority

    • Whether the to do is available for Private or Public viewing

    • Status

    • Additional notes

  3. Select the checkbox for Remind Me and enter a reminder date and time to receive a reminder of this activity.

  4. Select the Attendees tab and designate who will be responsible for the To Do.  You may select an entire Group or individual attendees.

    Note:     You can also track response to your email invitation through the Attendees tab.

  5. Select the Resources tab to reserve any resources, for example, a conference room or an overhead projector, that you require for your To Do.

  6. Select the Availability tab to check the availability of the participants.

  7. Select the Recurring tab to schedule an ongoing To Do .  An ongoing To Do is one that occurs daily, weekly, monthly, yearly or at some other frequency.

  8. Select the Attachments tab to send a file, either from the CentraView file system or from your desktop, along with the appointment notification.  You can send files as attachments or links.

  9. Select the Save and Close button to save the To Do or select the Save and New button to save and add another To Do .
