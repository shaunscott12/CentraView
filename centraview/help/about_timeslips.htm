About Timeslips

Business projects or support come with an associated cost: worker hours.  In order to keep costs and hours in line, CentraView allows you to easily transmit employee hours to your Accounting department.  Employee hours sent with the Timeslips function are automatically linked to the project code, if necessary, allowing you to spend more time working and less time concerned with budgets and invoices.

With CentraView, you can

  • Add timeslips

  • View timeslips

  • Delete timeslips

  • Duplicate timeslips

For an example of how time slips work with the rest of the CentraView functionality, see How do I use CentraView?
