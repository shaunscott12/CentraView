About Preferences

With CentraView, you can administer your own user preferences, determining a personal look and feel for your home page, email client and calendar, as well as general preferences, such as your user profile or synchronization information.

The General preferences area is where you set your personal contact information, for example, name, email address, phone numbers, user ID and password, synchronize your data or assign default privileges.

The Home Page preferences area allows you to determine which information displays on your CentraView home page.  You can choose to display important items, for example, your email folders, your calendar, your unscheduled activities or company news, as well as determining how often your home page information will refresh.

The Email preferences area allows you to manage your multiple email accounts, adding, editing or removing email addresses from CentraView.  You can also choose composition preferences, such as plain text or HTML, as well as setting a time interval for mail auto-checking.

The Calendar preferences are allows you to set the default view for your calendar, for example, a daily view, a weekly view or a monthly view, as well as determining how often your calendar information will refresh.  You can also grant delegation authority for someone besides yourself to schedule activities to your calendar.

To access the Preferences functionality, select the icon from the top menu bar.
